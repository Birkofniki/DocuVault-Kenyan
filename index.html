Problem: In today's world, we still rely on hard copies of important documents like IDs, passports, certificates, etc. This leads to numerous  problems:

  • Carrying around physical documents is inconvenient and they can easily be lost, damaged or stolen.
  • Verifying identity or qualifications with paper documents is tedious.
  • Sharing or transmitting documents digitally is difficult.
  • Managing and organizing a large number of physical documents is a challenge.
